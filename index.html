My Story

I've been working in the tech industry for 13 years, and in customer and client-facing roles for 15 years. Customer and client-facing roles gave me the ability to see things from their perspective. To advocate for the customer and client. To understand their struggles, issues and needs.

I'm currently working a multi-faceted role at a startup in Washington, DC. My role includes NOC/SaaS platform analysis and administration, direct client and 3PL software support analysis, as well as supporting roles for DevOps, product development and engineering and client solutions and implementations teams.

My goal is to take this experience and turn it into a role working as a UX/UI designer or engineer or as a systems analyst. I am currently finishing a bachelor of science degree from Arizona State University in the Technical Communication-User Experience program, with a minor in Applied Business Data Analytics.

This website serves as my outlet to share my work and story, as well as track my development in web design. This is version 2.0 of my site, developed from raw HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It is my way of forcing myself to learn web development.
